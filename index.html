How Questivate Works

About Questivate

Questivate was created to solve a common problem in education: the lack of effective student feedback mechanisms. We believe that students should have a voice in their education, and teachers should have access to the insights they need to improve.

By combining anonymous feedback with an engaging game experience, we've created a system that students actually want to use.

Our mission is to improve education through better communication and feedback.
